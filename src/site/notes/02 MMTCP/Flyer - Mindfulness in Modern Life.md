# 🧘 Mindfulness in Modern Life: An Invitation to Practice

### A Four-Week Introduction to Mindfulness Meditation

**In fulfillment of the requirements for MMTCP (Mindfulness Meditation Teacher Certification Program)**

---

## Join Us for a Journey Into Presence

Are you living a full, complex life and looking for a steady, accessible approach to mindfulness? This four-week course offers a gentle doorway into practice that meets you exactly where you are.

**Whether you're brand new to meditation or looking to deepen an existing practice, you are welcome here.**

## What You'll Experience

Each session includes:
- Two 20-minute guided meditation periods
- A 15-minute teaching on foundational mindfulness skills
- 15-minute Q&A and group discussion

### Course Content

**Week 1: What Is Mindfulness?**
Foundations of mindfulness, attitudinal qualities, breath and body as anchors.

**Week 2: Mindfulness of the Body and Breath**
Embodiment, grounding, meeting discomfort, stability through awareness.

**Week 3: Mettā and the Brahmavihāras**
Heart practices as support for mindfulness; cultivating friendliness and care.

**Week 4: Integration**
Bringing together mindfulness, embodiment, and heart practices; supporting continuity.

**Bonus: Optional Week 5**
"Continuing the Journey" — a community-guided session for ongoing practice and conversation.

---

## About the Course

This course is designed for everyday people living full, complex lives. You'll learn:
- Simple, sustainable ways to bring mindfulness into daily routines
- How to meet your experience with curiosity and kindness
- Foundational skills for awareness of breath and body
- Heart practices (mettā) that naturally support attention and emotional regulation

**The tone is secular, trauma-sensitive, and invitational.** Rather than striving for particular states, we encourage meeting yourself as you are and discovering what works for your life.
