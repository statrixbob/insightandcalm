# **📋 DAILY PODIUM iPAD CHECKLIST**

*(Run this each morning before the first session)*

### **1\. Power & Physical Setup**

-   iPad plugged into a **20W+ USB‑C charger**
    
-   Cable routed safely so it can’t be kicked or tugged
    
-   Case lying flat and stable on the slanted podium
    
-   Screen cleaned (fingerprints matter under stage lights)
    

### **2\. Display & Brightness**

-   **Auto‑Lock → Never**
    
-   **Auto‑Brightness → Off**
    
-   **True Tone → Off**
    
-   **Brightness** set to a stable, readable level
    
-   **Orientation Lock** set (portrait or landscape depending on your layout)
    

### **3\. Battery & System**

-   **Low Power Mode → Off**
    
-   **Optimized Battery Charging → On**
    
-   **Wi‑Fi connected** to the ballroom network
    
-   **Bluetooth Off** (unless needed)
    
-   **Do Not Disturb / Focus → On**
    
-   **Notifications → Off** for all apps
    

### **4\. App / Document Setup**

-   Open the **Google Doc** for the podium book
    
-   Zoom to a comfortable reading size
    
-   Scroll a bit to confirm smoothness
    
-   Confirm that edits made from another device appear within a few seconds
    
-   Optional: switch to **Safari** if you prefer the cleaner interface
    

### **5\. Guided Access (Scroll‑Only Mode)**

-   Triple‑click top button → **Start Guided Access**
    
-   **Options:**
    
    -   Touch → **On**
        
    -   Motion → **Off**
        
    -   Keyboard → **Off**
        
    -   Time Limit → **Off**
        
-   Confirm you cannot exit the app
    
-   Confirm you *can* scroll
    

### **6\. Final On‑Stage Check**

-   Podium height adjusted
    
-   iPad angle comfortable
    
-   Cable slack available
    
-   Presenter/moderator shown how to scroll
    
-   Confirm they know: “If anything changes, it will update automatically.”
