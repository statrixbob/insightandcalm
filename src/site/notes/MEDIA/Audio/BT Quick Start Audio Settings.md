## 🧘 Hybrid Dharma "Quick Start" Checklist

### 1. Hardware & Routing

- [ ] **RODECaster:** Ensure it's the primary input. (Your "Theater Voice" check).
    
- [ ] **Meeting Owl 3:** Verify as the **Audio Monitor** (Room Speaker).
    
- [ ] **The TV:** Confirm it’s showing the guest's face (but **muted** to avoid the Owl echo).
    

### 2. The "Amy Voice" Rescue (Ecamm Filters)

- [ ] **AUHighPassFilter:** Set to **100Hz**.
    
- [ ] **AUGraphicEQ:** Apply the "Warmth & Clarity" Preset.
    
    - _Quick Look:_ Mud cut (**-20dB**), Warmth nudge (**+3dB** at 250Hz), Clarity nudge (**+2dB** at 2.5kHz).
        
- [ ] **AUPeakLimiter:** Set to **+10dB Pre-gain** | **2ms Attack** | **40ms Release**.
    

### 3. The Human Element

- [ ] **The Phone Check:** Gently confirm the guest's phone is on "Do Not Disturb" (avoiding the "surprise notification" cameo).
    
- [ ] **Audio Levels:** Have the guest talk for 10 seconds to ensure the **+10dB boost** isn't hitting red on your Ecamm meters.
    

### 4. The Transitions (Stream Deck)

- [ ] **Scene 1 (Live):** YouTube/Ecamm active; Guest audio routed to the Owl.
    
- [ ] **Scene 2 (Q&A):** **Stop Stream.** Flip to the Owl's 360° mics for the private circle.
    

---

> **Pro-Tip:** If the room sounds "boxy" or echoey during the talk, give the **125Hz–160Hz** faders on the Graphic EQ a tiny nudge down.
